## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 The Problem: Cognitive Impedance Mismatch (CIM)
In hybrid human-AI collectives, a fundamental friction emerges when information generation velocity exceeds social processing bandwidth. This protocol formalizes the transition from synchronous consensus (Peeragogy) to asynchronous stigmergic coordination (Pyragogy) to prevent system collapse.

### 1.2 The Innovation: v1.1 Corneli Update
The 1.1 update introduces a formal taxonomy of interaction modes, allowing agents to switch protocols based on the "topological" nature of the collaboration, rather than just the technical load.

---

## 2. MATHEMATICAL FORMALIZATION

### 2.1 Cognitive Impedance ($Z_c$)
$$Z_c = \frac{V_{generation}}{B_{social}}$$

Where:
- $V_{g}$ = Velocity of information (bits/complexity/novelty).
- $B_{s}$ = Social bandwidth (human processing capacity/availability).

**Thresholds:**
- $Z_c < 0.7$: **Peeragogy Mode** (Synchronous consensus).
- $Z_c \geq 1.0$: **Pyragogy Mode** (Activate CRDT Bridge).

---

## 3. OPERATIONAL PRIMITIVES (MODIFIED v1.1)

### 3.1 Semantic Flow Operators
To manage the flow of convergence and divergence, the protocol utilizes two primary operators:

1.  **GUSH (Convergent):** Signal for rapid synchronization. *"I understand, I agree, move forward."* Used to minimize redundancy when the frame is stable.
2.  **BHO (Divergent/Resilient):** Signal for *Sustainable Divergence*. *"I accept the current gap in understanding to preserve the rhythm."* Prevents deadlock when $Z_c > 1.0$.

### 3.2 The CRDT Bridge
Non-destructive forking of cognitive states. When impedance is high, agents "fork" the discussion. Convergence is not forced but emerges stigmergically through shared traces in the environment.

## 5. ARCHITECTURAL PATTERN: THE BLUES PROTOCOL

**Definition:** *Asynchronous Call-and-Response.*
In high-impedance states, the response $R(t)$ does not need to match the semantic complexity of the call $S(t)$. Instead, it must match the **temporal interval** $\Delta t$ to maintain the "Keep-Alive" signal of the relationship.

* **Principle:** Rhythm over Logic.
* **Goal:** Maintain the "Groove" (Connection) while the background processing (Learning) catch up.
